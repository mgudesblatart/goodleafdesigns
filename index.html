About Me

I have been an armchair web...dabbler, for as long as I've been in school it seems. I started teaching myself coding when I was in middle school, and haven't shied away from it since.

I'm proficient in Photoshop and Illustrator. I am well versed in the basics, and am slowly but surely packing languages into my toolkit. I know HTML, CSS, jQuery, and most recently I've grown quite fond of PHP. I have worked with Joomla! and WordPress. Most recently, however, I have been learning and developing in Laravel 5.

The reason why I do any of this is because I love problem solving. I enjoy the high I get when I finally figure out how to do X or why Z isn't working. This is creative problem solving at its most immediate and rewarding.
